# Beyond Concierge — Executive OS · Demo Video Script

**Runtime:** 3–5 minutes · **Style:** SaaS product launch (Stripe / Linear feel)
**Recording:** Use the built-in **Guided Tour** (the "Take the tour" button) to auto-drive
the screens, or follow the shot list below with any screen recorder (Loom, ScreenStudio,
QuickTime). Music: minimal, warm, low-tempo. Cursor: smooth, deliberate.

> Tip: ScreenStudio or Loom at 1440×900, light cursor highlight, 1.0–1.1× zoom on numbers.

---

## 0:00 — Cold open (10s)
**Screen:** Executive Snapshot, full page, slow scroll from the hero quote down to the KPIs.
**VO:** "This is Beyond Concierge — not a dashboard, a command center. Every dollar, every
service, every channel… in one place."

## 0:10 — Module 1 · Executive Snapshot (35s)
**Screen:** Click the period toggle: This Month → This Quarter → This Year (numbers animate).
Hover the Revenue & Profit chart. Scroll to the P&L.
**VO:** "The morning view. Revenue by service line, a live P&L from gross all the way to net
profit, and twelve months of trend. Change the period and the whole business re-slices
instantly."

## 0:45 — Module 2 · Profit Engine (35s)
**Screen:** Click a few table rows (Botox, Memberships) — the cost waterfall updates. Sort by
Margin.
**VO:** "Here's where most med spas are flying blind. Every service, fully costed — product,
nurse, labor, even card fees — so you see *real* profit and margin. It auto-ranks your
winners, and flags anything thin."

## 1:20 — Module 3 · IV Recipe Calculator (35s)
**Screen:** Switch recipes (Myers' → Immunity). Tap +/− on Vitamin C. Drag the nurse-time
slider. Click **Dispense** — watch the stock bars drop and the activity log appear.
**VO:** "Build any drip and the margin calculates live. When you dispense, it draws the
ingredients straight out of inventory — no more guessing what a bag actually costs you."

## 1:55 — Module 4 · GLP-1 Margin Center (30s)
**Screen:** Show the cost-per-mg cards. Click a patient's "draw dose" button — vial mg drops.
**VO:** "Weight loss is the fastest-growing line, so we track it to the milligram. Cost per
mg, profit per patient, profit per vial — and every dose depletes the right lot in real time."

## 2:25 — Module 5 · Inventory Command (25s)
**Screen:** Low-stock alerts, expiry badges, the four mover cards, filter the table by category.
**VO:** "Live stock, reorder alerts, expiry watch, and the fast and slow movers that quietly
make — or cost — you money."

## 2:50 — Module 6 · Website & SEO (20s)
**Screen:** Traffic-by-source chart, keyword position changes, the prioritized fix-list.
**VO:** "Your storefront, measured. Traffic, rankings, Google Business Profile, and exactly
which pages to fix first."

## 3:10 — Module 7 · Social Command (25s)
**Screen:** Channel health cards, follower trend, the content calendar, then click
**Generate ideas** in the AI assistant.
**VO:** "Every channel's health, your content calendar, and an AI assistant that hands you
the next week of posts — hooks, reels, captions, and all."

## 3:35 — Module 8 · Advertising (20s)
**Screen:** ROAS bars by platform, the campaign table, the budget-move recommendations.
**VO:** "Every ad dollar traced to revenue — ROAS, cost per appointment, and a clear call on
where to move the budget."

## 3:55 — Module 9 · CRM & Partnerships (20s)
**Screen:** Click two partners to swap the detail panel; show follow-ups due.
**VO:** "The gyms, doctors, and influencers that send you patients — ranked by the revenue
they actually drive, with follow-ups you won't miss."

## 4:15 — Module 10 · 90-Day Growth Plan (15s)
**Screen:** Check off a task — the phase progress and overall bar move.
**VO:** "A quarter of operating cadence: foundation, awareness, scale. Check it off as you go."

## 4:30 — AI Advisor · The close (25s)
**Screen:** The Weekly Executive Brief card, slow scroll through Issues → Opportunities →
Recommendations.
**VO:** "And on top of it all, an advisor reading every module at once — your immediate
issues, your biggest opportunities, and an executive brief, written for you every week.
This is Beyond Concierge: the single source of truth for the whole business."

**End card (5s):** Logo + "Beyond Concierge · Executive OS" on charcoal, gold underline.
